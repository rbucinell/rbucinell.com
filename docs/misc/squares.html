Bucinell Superbowl Squares Super Bowl LVI (2022)

  1. Home / 
  2. Super Bowl Squares

It's that time of year again. Superbowl, baby! Last year we ran squares for Ryder's shower, but this year all money is on the table. There are new rules this year, and a new table. Good Luck.

Rules

  • Squares are $10. Please Paypal or venmo me at @rbucinell
  • The Pot will consist of the sum of the paid squres
  • The numbers will be randomized 30mins prior to the game start.
  • Board filled and paid up, numbers rolled by RANDOM.ORG
  • Payouts willl ramp up each quarter. Here are the pertentage payouts for each quarter:
    • End of 1st: 10%
    • End of 2nd: 20%
    • End of 3rd: 30%
    • Final Score: 40%
  • The winning square will be determined by the sum of the digits of the teams' score. (e.g. NFC Champion has 17 at the end of the first, the winning number will be 8 [1+7] )
  • I reserve the right to remove unpaid square request.
  • Unclaimed/unpaid squares that win, will splash the payout to the cardinal 4 directions in an equal payout (recursively until all money has been distributed)
  • If you have any problems picking squares, please text Jess or I. If you don't specify a square, or the square requested is taken we will pick the next sequentially availiable
