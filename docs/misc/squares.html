For COVID-19 safety precautions we are not going to hold a traditional baby sprinkle or a large Super Bowl party. However that isn't going to stop the fun. We are going to host a Super Bowl squares where the proceeds will go to the only thing on our registry: The WonderFold Wagon W4S 2.0 so that we can push the entire clan around at the same time. Thank you so much for all who play and help us with this goal. Good Luck.

Rules

  • Squares are $20. Please Paypal or venmo me at @rbucinell
  • The Pot will consist of the sum of the paid squres minus the MSRP of the wagon
  • The numbers will be randomized. They will be selected at 6pm on the 7th.
  • Payouts willl ramp up each quarter. Here are the pertentage payouts for each quarter:
    • End of 1st: 10%
    • End of 2nd: 20%
    • End of 3rd: 30%
    • Final Score: 40%
  • I reserve the right to remove unpaid square request.
  • If you have any problems picking squares, please text Jess or I. If you don't specify a square, or the square requested is taken we will pick the next sequentially availiable
