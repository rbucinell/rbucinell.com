About me

I am a software engineer from Rochester, NY. I'm not native to the area, however after receiving my bachelor's of science in computer science from Rochester Institute of Technology I decided to stick around for the warm winters and garbage plates.

I have a wide array of hobbies to keep me busy, while on my computer I enjoy playing video games, coding my own personal projects, and helping/tutoring other new programmers, and re-binge-watching TV series on Netflix. When I get out I enjoy walking my dog, playing (and refereeing) kickball in some bar leagues. I also love to do mud runs, which drive me into the gym to train for them. I have completed multiple Tough Mudders, Spartan Sprints, Hero Rush, and Warrior Dashes.

I am happily married to my beautiful wife, Jessica, and we are currently expecting twins in December of 2019. So if our dog wasn't enough to keep us busy, then my first two kids will surely fill my time and create all new adventures.
